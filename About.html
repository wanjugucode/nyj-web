primary school in Mukurwe-ini and after my K.C.P.E, I joined Gatugi Girl High school
in Othaya and completed my secondary school education in Giathugu secondary school
in Mukurwe-ini . In 2021 I enrolled in the codeHive program which is now ongoing.

I am a kind hearted caring individual that looks out for other,I am able to keep calm and
level head whenever am in a situation of stress.I would like to consider myself as someone
who can relate to anyone and any culture and background.It allows me to make very personal connection with everyone I meet and lastly I would say I am quiet in crowd but not when I am
one on one with a person.

My hobbies are reading, writing, dancing ,swimming, and exploring.
