BLOG 1

ABEL MARRYING PATRICIA

"Abel wanted a traditional marriage with a traditional wife, I wondered why he ever
married a woman like my mom in the first place, as she was the opposite of that in
every way...", Trevor says.

In the book BORN A CRIME by Trevor Noah, we see that Abel was a traditional man
and he made a mistake to marry a woman who was not ready to make a subservient, she
was an independent, modern woman, and this was the reason why they never got along.
Abel was only worried about what his friends and his family would say. He didn’t care
about the well-being of his family. He wanted to impress his friends, family, and
everyone else instead of adjusting to living in harmony with his wife.

He was indeed a dictator and a traditional man whereas Patricia was the opposite of that.
Patricia did not also make the right decision. I am very sure that she knew the dark side
of Abel. she wouldn’t have given him hand in marriage to prevent the kind of drama she
experienced.

In real life, Abel and Patricia marriage is an example of how decision-making affects
our life. We should be able to see ahead and also if by any chance we make a wrong
decision we should not be too rigid,we should pivot and keep going since life is a battle,
we fight with imposter, nature, and failures but only the strong succeed

Humor helped Noah and his family heal for instance when his mother was shot and almost died. Noah says, "yeah even during this time, and I did not find any humor in that.
